📲 Install as an app
Add to home screen — no App Store.

Your progress is saved on this device, in this browser. Always open the app from the same browser — and avoid clearing your cache — to keep your sessions and streak intact.

Install the app
Add to your home screen
This app runs entirely in your browser — no App Store needed. Follow these steps to install it on your phone like a real app, with its own icon on your home screen.
📱 iPhone — Safari
1
Open the link in Safari
Make sure you're using Safari — not Chrome, Firefox or another browser. The install feature only works in Safari on iPhone.
2
Tap the Share button
At the bottom of Safari, tap the Share icon — the square with an arrow pointing up.
3
Tap "Add to Home Screen"
Scroll down in the share sheet and tap "Add to Home Screen".
4
Tap "Add" — done ✓
The app icon appears on your home screen and launches in full screen.
🤖 Android — Chrome
1
Open the link in Chrome
Use Google Chrome on your Android phone.
2
Tap the menu (⋮)
Tap the three dots in the top-right corner of Chrome.
3
Tap "Add to Home screen"
Select "Add to Home screen" or "Install app". Confirm and the icon appears.
✓ Your progress is saved automatically
The app saves your profile, session completions, feedbacks and weekly check-ins to your phone's local storage.

Calendar export
Add your program to your calendar
Export all 10 weeks of sessions directly to your calendar — so your runs, Pilates and rest days appear automatically alongside the rest of your life.
Step 1 — Export the .ics file
1
Go to Program → Download
Tap the "Download your program" banner at the bottom of the Program tab.
2
Set your start date and session time
Choose when your program begins and what time sessions are scheduled by default.
3
Tap "Export .ics file"
A file will download. Each event includes the session title, description, duration, and a 30-minute reminder.
Step 2 — Import into your calendar
📱 Apple Calendar (iPhone / Mac)
iPhone: Files app → find the .ics file → tap it → tap "Add All".
Mac: Double-click the .ics file — Calendar opens automatically.
🗓 Google Calendar
On desktop: calendar.google.com → ⚙ → Settings → Import & export → Import.
📧 Outlook
File → Open & Export → Import/Export → Import an iCalendar (.ics) file.
✓ What's included in each event
Session title and type · Phase of the program · Full description · Duration · A 30-minute reminder.
💡 Tip: If you change your start date or race date later, re-export the .ics and re-import it. Delete the old events first.
