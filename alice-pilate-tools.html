How to Use
Everything you need in 2 minutes
01
Choose your programme
0 to 10K or 10K to Half Marathon. Pick your level, set your start date, and you are ready.
02
Follow Today
Open the app each day. Your session is right there — Pilates, running, or rest. Tap Done, Partial, or Skip when finished. Changed your mind? Tap Change to undo.
03
Weekly check-in
At the end of each week, answer 3 quick questions on pain, fatigue, and motivation. The app adapts your next week if needed.
04
Programme view
See your full 10-week plan week by week. Navigate with the arrows to preview what is coming.
05
Pace calculator
Enter any recent race time and distance. The app calculates your semi-marathon pace, easy pace, and interval pace instantly.
06
Calendar export
In Settings, export your full programme to Apple Calendar, Google Calendar, or Outlook as a .ics file.
07
Streaks and badges
Complete your weekly check-in to build your streak. Badges unlock as you progress through each phase.
